Vision and Mission!

We have two main objectives, one is to remember our dearest friends always as they were very talented and bright students, If they are here with us physically, they must be reached top level with their respective streams of Medicine and Engineering. The other main objective is to help students whoever needs financial support to continue their studies and as well as to help our school in different ways..
